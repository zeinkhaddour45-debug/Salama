About

What the Counseling Center Does

The AUB Counseling Center offers free, confidential psychological support to all enrolled students. Services focus on short-term individual counseling to help students navigate personal challenges, academic stress, anxiety, grief, relationship difficulties, and more.

The Center's approach encourages students to build self-awareness, develop coping strategies, and strengthen their independence. When longer-term or specialized care is needed, counselors can refer students to appropriate services within AUBMC.

Services

What's Available

  • Individual short-term counseling sessions
  • Crisis intervention and urgent support
  • Referrals to psychiatry or specialist care when needed
  • Workshops and group sessions on mental wellbeing
  • Consultations for faculty and staff who are concerned about a student
