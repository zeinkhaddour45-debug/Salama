About

Who Are the Deputies?

Title IX Deputies are faculty and staff members embedded across AUB's schools, faculties, and units. They are trained and certified by the Association of Title IX Administrators (ATIXA) and can receive reports of discrimination or harassment, provide guidance on the process, and connect you with the Title IX Coordinator. You can approach the deputy in your own unit or any other deputy you feel comfortable with.
