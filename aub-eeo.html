Office of Equal Opportunity & Title IX

Students · Faculty · Staff

AUB's Title IX office handles reports of discrimination and discriminatory harassment, including sexual harassment. Everyone at AUB has the right to study and work free from discrimination.

About

AUB's Commitment to Equal Opportunity

AUB is committed to the principle of equal opportunity in admission, education, and employment. Consistent with this commitment and with Lebanese, U.S., and other applicable laws, it is the policy of AUB not to tolerate discrimination or discriminatory harassment of any kind.

In alignment with Title IX of the U.S. Education Amendments of 1972, which prohibits sex discrimination in educational settings, AUB maintains a Title IX Coordinator in the Office of the President. The office is supported by a network including an Advisory Council, Title IX Deputies, and qualified Panel Members for formal investigations, all certified by the Association of Title IX Administrators (ATIXA).

Scope

What This Office Covers

  • Discrimination based on sex, gender, race, religion, nationality, or other protected characteristics
  • Sexual harassment and sexual misconduct
  • Discriminatory harassment in academic or employment settings
  • Retaliation against those who raise concerns in good faith
