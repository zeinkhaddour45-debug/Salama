About

What the Department Offers

The Department of Psychiatry at AUBMC provides specialist psychological and psychiatric support for AUB students, faculty, and staff. Those enrolled in AUB's Health Insurance Plan (HIP) are typically referred through UHS, while individuals not covered by HIP may contact the department directly.

The department offers a full continuum of psychiatric care, from routine outpatient visits through to emergency and inpatient services when required.
