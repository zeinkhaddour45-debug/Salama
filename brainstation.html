Brainstation Clinics is a leading mental health network in Lebanon with an expanding international presence, dedicated to making evidence-based psychological care accessible while breaking the stigma around mental health in the Arab world. Brainstation offers a wide range of clinical services delivered by a multidisciplinary team of highly trained therapists, specializing in CBT, DBT, ACT, Schema-Focused CBT, IPT, and other scientific approaches.

Alongside therapy, Brainstation is a center for education and professional development, providing workshops, accredited training programs, and continuous supervision for mental health professionals. Brainstation is also committed to advancing psychology through its Research Department, collaborating with universities and institutions to generate scientific insights and improve clinical practice.

With its three pillars clinical care, education, and research Brainstation Clinics stands as a trusted hub for mental health, empowering individuals, professionals, and communities to thrive.
