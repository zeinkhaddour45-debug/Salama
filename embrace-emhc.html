The Mental Health Clinic consists of an experienced team of mental health professionals who offer quality mental health services to children and adults, without any barriers. The clinic welcomes anyone who may be depressed, moody, anxious, or has gone through trauma or grief, relationship or family problems, along with other concerns. Services are offered at low fees, set to be affordable so that everyone can access the care they need.

What We Offer

Types of Services

  • Psychotherapy using cognitive-behavioral therapy, family-systems, and brief psychodynamic approaches
  • Psychiatric consultations where medical doctors assess, counsel, and prescribe medical treatments
  • Group therapy sessions where professionals help several people at once
  • Psychosocial consultations to connect people with services in the community, and access free medications, shelter, and protection from harm

Training

Clinical Training Program

The program is modeled after guidelines published by the American Psychological Association (APA) and adapted to local resources and capacities. It aims to meet and exceed the minimum training requirements set by Lebanese Law 8 regarding hours of observation and training for psychologists seeking licensure in Lebanon.

Duration 11 months (September 1 – July 30)
Modality On-site (partially online), ~13–16 hrs/week
