Championing Mental Health
as a Basic Right

IDRAAC believes mental health is a fundamental human right. Through advocacy and awareness, we work to break stigma, influence policy, and promote well-being across all communities and age groups in Lebanon.

Our Initiatives

What We Do

1

Public Awareness Campaigns

Nationwide campaigns using social media, community outreach, and traditional media to educate the public, reduce stigma, and encourage open conversations about mental health.

2

Policy Advocacy

Collaborating with policymakers and healthcare professionals to integrate mental health into national health policies, contributing research-based recommendations for better access to services.

3

Community Engagement & Training

Workshops and awareness sessions for schools, workplaces, and community groups, equipping individuals with the knowledge and tools to support mental health in their environments.

4

Media & Educational Resources

Expert insights and practical tips shared through TV, radio, podcasts, and online content, making accurate mental health information accessible to all.

5

Support for Vulnerable Groups

Tailored awareness programs for older adults, refugees, caregivers, and frontline workers, addressing their unique mental health challenges and ensuring no one is left behind.

Join Us in Raising Awareness

Everyone has a role to play. Whether you're an individual, an organization, or a policymaker, you can support IDRAAC's mission by sharing resources, attending events, or partnering with us to build a mentally healthier society.
