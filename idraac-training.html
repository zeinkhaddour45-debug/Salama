Training Programs

At IDRAAC, we offer a variety of specialized training programs aimed at enhancing mental health knowledge and skills across different sectors. Our comprehensive trainings are designed to support workplace wellness, empower educators, and provide essential skills for caregivers. Through these programs, we strive to foster a more informed and supportive community for mental health.

What We Offer

Specialized Training Programs

Mental Health in the Workplace Training

Workplace

IDRAAC offers specialized training sessions designed to improve mental health awareness and support within the workplace. These sessions cover topics such as stress management, coping mechanisms, and creating a supportive work environment.

Training for Educators

Education

Our training programs for educators focus on identifying and addressing mental health issues in students. Educators learn to recognize early signs of mental distress and are equipped with strategies to support and refer students in need.

Vocational Training for Caregivers of Alzheimer's Patients

Caregiving

IDRAAC provides vocational training tailored for caregivers of Alzheimer's patients. This training includes techniques for managing the unique challenges of caregiving, enhancing communication skills, and ensuring the well-being of both the caregiver and the patient.
