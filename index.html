Who We Are

Our Mission

At Salama, our mission is to make mental health accessible, relatable, and stigma-free for students and the wider Lebanese community.

We aim to create a safe digital space where anyone can learn, share, and seek support through educational content, personal stories, and trusted resources.

Built by university students, Salama bridges the gap between awareness and action, helping every individual feel seen, understood, and empowered to care for their mental well-being.

We believe that mental health should be spoken about openly, without shame or judgment. Through community, education, and compassion, Salama strives to normalize conversations around mental well-being and create a culture where seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.
