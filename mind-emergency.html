24/7
Emergency Hotline Available Around the clock, every day of the year
At MIND, mental health emergencies can occur at any time. That is why MIND provides a 24/7 hotline for emergency calls and requests, ensuring that help is always just a phone call away. Each unit has a dedicated hotline where patients can reach out to MIND's professionals for immediate assistance, and the multidisciplinary team is ready to respond to emergency needs.

In addition to hotline services, MIND offers emergency medical care and psychiatric services at the emergency department and inpatient facility located at St. Georges Hospital University Medical Center. Resident doctors are available around the clock to provide prompt and comprehensive care for those in need.
