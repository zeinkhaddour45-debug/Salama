MIND offers outpatient services across multiple locations in Lebanon and soon regionally, providing convenient access to comprehensive mental health care. MIND's outpatient services are distinguished by a multidisciplinary approach, led by a team of skilled psychologists, psychiatrists, psychiatric nurses, and assistants.

Whether you require immediate support or ongoing care, MIND's dedicated team is available to assist. Delivering the highest quality of care is central to everything MIND does.
