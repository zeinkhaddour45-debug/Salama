Inpatient Services

Inpatient

Lebanon's first psychiatric care unit within a general hospital, at St Georges Hospital UMC, offering over 40 years of integrated, evidence-based inpatient treatment.

Learn more →

Home Diagnosis & Treatment

Home Care

In-home mental health care by psychologists, psychiatrists, and clinical assistants, for individuals who are unable to visit a clinic in person.

Learn more →

Online Diagnosis & Treatment

Online

The region's first medical center to offer full telepsychiatry and telepsychology, initial consultations, follow-ups, and second opinions from anywhere.

Learn more →

International Consultations

International

Expert psychiatric and psychological second opinions for patients and families based outside Lebanon, delivered remotely by MIND's senior specialists.

Learn more →

Interventional Psychiatry

Interventional

Advanced brain stimulation and neuromodulation therapies, including ECT, ketamine infusion, and rTMS, for treatment-resistant conditions.

24h Emergency Services

Round-the-clock psychiatric emergency care and a 24/7 hotline, because mental health emergencies can occur at any time.
