On Campus

University Support

Support services available within your campus, offering accessible and confidential care for students.

AUB Student Mental Health & Wellbeing

AUB

Free, confidential counseling for AUB students. Individual sessions, group therapy, and crisis support available through the Student Affairs office.

LAU Counseling Center

LAU

Professional counseling services for LAU students on both Beirut and Byblos campuses. Short-term individual therapy and referral support.

NDU Psychological Services

NDU

Psychological counseling for Notre Dame University students. Offers individual consultations and group support in a safe, confidential environment.

USJ Student Services

USJ

Saint Joseph University's Counseling Office (SAP) supports students experiencing emotional or psychological distress, staffed by qualified psychologists and psychiatrists.

Accessible Care

Free & Low-Cost Support

Organizations providing mental health support at little to no cost, making care accessible when you need it most.

Embrace Lebanon

Free

Lebanon's leading mental health NGO dedicated to suicide prevention and emotional wellbeing. Offers free counseling, community programs, and a crisis hotline (1564).

IDRAAC

Free

Institute for Development, Research, Advocacy and Applied Care. A leading mental health NGO in Lebanon offering free psychosocial support, therapy, and public education.

Restart Center

Low-cost

Specializes in supporting survivors of trauma, torture, and displacement. Offers free or sliding-scale psychological therapy and rehabilitation services.

MIND Clinics

Clinical

A multidisciplinary mental health clinic with several locations across Lebanon, offering psychiatric, psychological, and neuropsychological services for all age groups.

Ongoing Care

Professional Support

Connect with licensed professionals and clinics for ongoing, specialized mental health care.

AUB Medical Center Psychiatry

Clinical

The Department of Psychiatry at AUBMC offers specialist outpatient consultations, emergency psychiatric assessment, and inpatient care. Available to AUB students, faculty, and staff, with referral through UHS or direct contact for those outside the health insurance plan.

Brainstation Psychotherapy & Training

Clinical

A Beirut-based psychotherapy center offering individual and group therapy, alongside professional training programs in evidence-based mental health practices.

Crisis Support

Immediate Help

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out now. These services are available to support you through the hardest moments.

Embrace Lebanon
Lebanon's first dedicated suicide prevention hotline. Available daily 3 PM to 11 PM, staffed by trained counselors.
1564 embracelebanon.org →
IDRAAC Crisis Line
The IDRAAC helpline connects you with a counselor for immediate emotional support and guidance to appropriate services.
01 202 220 idraac.org →
Lebanese Red Cross
For medical emergencies and immediate physical safety. The Red Cross also provides first psychological aid in crisis situations.
140 redcross.org.lb →
General Emergency
For any life-threatening emergency in Lebanon. Do not hesitate to call if you or someone else is in immediate danger.
112 Available 24/7
