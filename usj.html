Student Services

Saint Joseph University of Beirut offers a range of mental health and wellbeing resources for students. Below is an overview of the services available to you.

Office locations and contact details may change over time. Contact each service directly for the most up-to-date information.

Resources at USJ

Counseling Services (SAP)

Free

The Counseling Office (SAP) provides confidential psychological and psychiatric support to students experiencing emotional or psychological distress. Staffed by qualified psychologists and psychiatrists, it is part of the Department of Psychology at the Faculty of Humanities.

Psychiatry & Mental Health

Hôtel-Dieu de France

USJ offers comprehensive mental health services through the Psychiatry Department at Hôtel-Dieu de France, providing specialist psychiatric and psychological care for students and the wider community.
